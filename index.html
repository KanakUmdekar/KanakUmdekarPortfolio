About Me

Hello! My name is Kanak Umdekar, hailing from the vibrant city of Bhopal. Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering from SIRT Bhopal. Academically, I have consistently excelled, securing 78% in 10th standard, 83.2% in 12th standard, and maintaining an aggregate score of 8.4 in my engineering studies. Driven by the belief that computer science is omnipresent, I have dedicated the past 3.5 years to honing my software skills. My technical arsenal includes proficiency in Python, C/C++, SQL, Microsoft Office, and more. Additionally, I am also familiar with web development. During this journey, I've leveraged these skills to develop various projects, ranging from creating a virtual assistant to conducting exploratory data analysis on COVID-19 datasets and tackling data cleaning projects. I've also actively participated in hackathons, further refining my abilities. Beyond the realm of technology, I find joy in music, particularly singing. I've served as a club coordinator in my institute's student club and volunteered with the Qayas Youth Foundation, contributing to their research initiatives. Characterized by optimism and a relentless pursuit of goals, I approach every endeavor with precision and a results-driven mindset. As I enter my final year of B.Tech, specializing in Computer Science, I am eager to apply my skills to real-world challenges and collaborate in dynamic environments. Thank you for taking the time to learn about me. Let's connect and explore opportunities for mutual growth and success!
